ABOUT US

OnePlus has evolved from a disruptive startup in 2014 with its "flagship killer" OnePlus One into a major global smartphone brand. Known for offering top-tier specs at competitive prices, it steadily moved into the premium segment with innovations like fast charging, high-refresh-rate displays, and partnerships with brands like Hasselblad. Its newer models, like the OnePlus 13, focus on AI enhancements and premium design, though rising prices and shorter software support cycles have drawn some criticism.Despite this, OnePlus remains strong in markets like India and Europe, offering a mix of performance, style, and value.
